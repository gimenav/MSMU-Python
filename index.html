Women in Tech

For centuries, humans have made new discoveries, they have created new products, and have come up with new ideas to solve problems. If we look back in time, we can think of a few great discoveries and creations, such as electricity, the DNA structure, penicillin, rocket ships and airplanes.

Fast forward to now, we have discoveries of Quantum Teleportation and gene therapy, and have creations like the Mars rover and robotic prosthetics. And these are only a handful of innovations that humans have achieved with the help of the STEM field, imagine what other fascinating things we can create together! So, join the STEM field, you can help to build the future!
